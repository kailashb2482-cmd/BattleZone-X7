Fair Play Policy
1. Our Commitment

BattleZone X is built on the foundation of fair competition. We are committed to ensuring that every player has an equal and honest opportunity to compete and win. Any form of cheating, exploitation, or manipulation will not be tolerated.

2. Anti-Cheat Measures

We employ a multi-layered anti-cheat system that includes:

  • Automated Result Verification: Match results are cross-verified with official game APIs and server data.
  • Screenshot Analysis: AI-powered tools analyze submitted screenshots for inconsistencies or manipulation.
  • Behavioral Monitoring: Unusual gameplay patterns (impossible kill counts, abnormal survival times) are flagged for review.
  • Player Reporting: Users can report suspected cheaters directly from the tournament results page.
3. Prohibited Activities
  • Teaming / Collusion: Working with players outside your designated team to gain an advantage in solo or duo tournaments.
  • Hacking / Modding: Using unauthorized third-party apps, mods, or hacks that alter gameplay.
  • Result Manipulation: Submitting forged, edited, or incorrect screenshots to influence tournament outcomes.
  • Smurfing: Playing in lower-tier tournaments using alternate accounts to dominate less experienced players.
  • Account Sharing: Allowing another player to play on your behalf in a tournament.
4. Penalties

Violations of the Fair Play Policy will result in:

  • First Offense: Warning and disqualification from the current tournament. Entry fee forfeited.
  • Second Offense: 7-day account suspension and forfeiture of winnings from the affected tournament.
  • Third Offense: Permanent ban from BattleZone X with full wallet balance forfeiture.
5. Dispute Resolution

If you believe you were wrongly penalized, you may submit an appeal within 48 hours of the decision. Our Fair Play team will review all evidence and make a final determination within 72 hours. All decisions are final.

6. Skill-Based Gaming

All tournaments on BattleZone X are skill-based. Outcomes depend primarily on the player's skill, strategy, and experience. The element of chance in battle royale games does not make these games "games of chance" under Indian law.

7. Contact

To report a violation or appeal a decision, contact fairplay@battlezonex.in.

BattleZone X provides refunds on tournament entry fees under the following conditions:

  • Tournament Cancellation: If BattleZone X cancels a tournament due to insufficient players, server issues, or any other reason, the full entry fee is automatically refunded to your wallet within 10 minutes.
  • Match Not Started: If the match room is created but the game never begins (due to server maintenance or technical failure), a full refund is issued.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

Entry fees are NOT refundable in the following cases:

  • You were disqualified for violating the Fair Play Policy.
  • Your internet connection dropped or your device had issues during the match.

Deposits made to your BattleZone X wallet can be refunded to your original payment source under the following conditions:
